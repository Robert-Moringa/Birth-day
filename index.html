Akan Name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. For example, a male child born on Sunday is called Kwasi while a female child born on the same day is called Akosua . Click here for more.
